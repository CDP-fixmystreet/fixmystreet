FixMyStreet for Councils

FixMyStreet.com is the UK's best-known fault-reporting website. Now the award-winning technology behind FixMyStreet is available for council websites.

Get in touch

Your residents, working for you

When even a small percentage of your residents have access to FixMyStreet from their smartphone, it makes a big difference.

Suddenly, you have eyes and ears everywhere, ready to report streetscene issues wherever they are encountered.

FixMyStreet turns those users into experts, filing reports with the information you need to get the problems fixed. Reports automatically include detailed location data. Residents may also add a photograph to help you identify and assess the issue.

Appy days

Your residents want to report faults as they see them, so FixMyStreet for Councils is available as an app for iPhones and Android.

Additionally, it can be accessed via any smartphone browser, as a mobile-optimised website.

But there may not be great data coverage in every part of your borough. That's fine: the FixMyStreet for Councils apps also allow users to make a report on site; it then saves it and sends it off once there is access to a connection.

Reports go directly into your systems

FixMyStreet for Councils can integrate directly, putting your user's reports directly into your system.

They'll go directly to the department who can best deal with them. Meanwhile, you can keep the resident informed with push notifications as the report goes through the fix cycle.

Just the way you like it

FixMyStreet for Councils is extremely flexible. You decide your report categories, and where those reports should be routed.

You can include a variety of report statuses, from 'in progress' to 'awaiting further information' to 'fixed'.

You can tell us which types of report are not your responsibility, and where we should route them instead.

You dictate the text that we display at every step (or use ours - we've worked hard on it!).

Bin those clunky forms

Why frustrate your users with unfriendly interfaces? Long forms are a big turn-off, and may be driving your residents to the phone.

You can bin clunky forms forever when you integrate FixMyStreet's elegant fault-reporting interface with your own site.

Stress-free hosting

FixMyStreet for Councils is hosted in the cloud, but is templated to look and feel exactly like a part of your website.

That makes upgrades and maintenance our business - and leaves you to get on with the business of getting things fixed.

Residents love it

We get a lot of love in the FixMyStreet mailbag - and now you can tap into some of that positive feeling too.

Why do people feel so strongly about FixMyStreet? Because it's an easy way for citizens to take control over the problems that affect them in their own neighbourhood.

Reports are published online as well as going into council channels, giving you a great way to display the previously hidden work you do - and providing residents with a forum for discussion.

Who's using FixMyStreet?

Our UK client councils include Stevenage, Bromley, Barnet, Oxfordshire, Hart, and (soon to launch) Liverpool City Council and Warwickshire County Council.

FixMyStreet for Councils is also in use outside the UK, in Zurich (Switzerland) and Cebu (Philippines).

Beyond councils

FixMyStreet is flexible software: it can be adapted for any use that relies on people pinpointing a location on a map. That might include crime reporting, bird spotting, or any other type of crowdsourced, map-based project.

For example, reporting empty homes (an award-winning website and iPhone app for Channel 4 and the Empty Homes Agency); anti-social behaviour (See Something Say Something, an iPhone and Android app for the West Midlands Safer Travel Partnership); vaccine stock shortage (a website, currently in development, for reporting when TB clinics in Delhi have run out of medicine).

Tell us a novel use for FixMyStreet for Councils, and we bet we can make it happen.
